March 2025

What I learned building a real database from scratch

In OMIS 105 we designed a full relational database for a local coffee shop. The hardest part wasn't SQL — it was the ER modeling stage. Getting the entities right before touching a single line of code determined everything downstream. Third normal form sounds abstract until you realize a bad schema means rewriting every query.

August 2025

Content migration at scale: what 1,000 pages teaches you about QA

At AARP I worked across WordPress, AEM, and DAM systems migrating thousands of pages of content. The bottleneck was never the migration itself — it was QA. Broken links and misformatted assets compound fast. Building a repeatable checklist early, and flagging issues before publication, was the difference between a 20% efficiency gain and a nightmare rollback.
